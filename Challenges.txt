3. What is fault tolerance ? Fault tolerance in distributed systems is the ability to maintain continuous operation and service despite failures (hardware, software, network) in one or more components, ensuring high availability, reliability, and data integrity through strategies like redundancy, replication, and error detection to prevent single points of failure

If a concept can change over time, it does NOT belong in an identity enum.
